5. Featured Project – Learning Management System (LMS)

We specialize in developing and integrating Learning Management Systems (LMS) for educational institutions, corporate training programs, and skill development organizations.

Key Features of Our LMS Solution

✔ Web Dashboard – Centralized management for courses, users, payments, and reports.
✔ Tutor App – Allows instructors to create and manage courses.
✔ Student App – Enables learners to access courses, live classes, and assignments.
✔ Live & Pre-Recorded Sessions – Seamless integration with Zoom/Google Meet.
✔ Automated Assessments & Certification – Digital grading, quizzes, and certifications.
✔ Payment Gateway Integration – Supports online course purchases.
✔ Discussion Forums & Chat Features – Encourages collaboration and interaction.
✔ Progress Tracking & Analytics – Provides insights into student performance.

Our LMS solutions are fully customizable, ensuring they align with your organization’s learning goals and objectives.
